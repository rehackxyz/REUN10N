Girls in CTF 2024

The second Girls in CTF was held in October 2024. Organized by RE:UN10N members, led by D4RkN1GHT, GCTF2024 introduced new categories such as DevSecOps and Smart Contract (by X3 Security).

The prizes for GCTF2024 were sponsored by Pentesterlab, OffSec, and some anonymous individuals, including:

  • 1st place: Super special gifts + GCTF2024 Tshirt + PentesterLab 3 months Premium + SEC-100 by OffSec
  • 2nd place: Special gifts + GCTF2024 Tshirt + PentesterLab 3 months Premium
  • 3rd place: Gifts + GCTF2024 Tshirt + PentesterLab 3 months Premium
  • Best writeups: GCTF2024 Tshirt + PentesterLab 3 months Premium
